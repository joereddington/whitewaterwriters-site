The Games: The Fight for Freedom

Dear Contestants, If you want to survive The Game, you will have to endure a world of mad scientists, crazed monsters and serial killers with split personalities. The world is brutal. The people are insane, and the monsters are cruel. Whose side will our heroes take in a world in which what was right died when the wall went up. Or will they perish with the rest? So. Do you want to play?
