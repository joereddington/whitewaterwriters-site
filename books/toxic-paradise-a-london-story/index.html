Toxic Paradise: A London Story

Tyreece is a conflicted young musician in a world without rhythm. The unholy trinity of drugs, money and alcohol aren't the only thing to influence his life. But there is one shining light in his life, the beat of the drums like an echo in his heart. Forced to leave the lush countryside of Somerset for the dense forest of tower blocks in Hackney, where both love and hate thrive, Tyreece finds solace and hope in the most unexpected places. He learns that life is to be lived in full joy, and that anyone can change their future by making a difference in the here and now.
