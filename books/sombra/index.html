Sombra

Sombra (meaning shadow in Spanish) is a story about a 15 year old expert hacker, Alex, and the adventures he goes on after losing all forms of identification while on the run from gangs and agents. He meets his friend in Cairo and some unfriendly pirates on a stolen cargo ship. After several encounters and challenges from the sandy deserts of Cairo to the busy streets of India, Alex arrives back home safely in China.
