Glenburn Sports College - Memories

On the dark hills around the Skelmersdale Beacon lurks a creature. It preys on the unsuspecting, stealing their memories in it’s search for answers. But it’s taken one memory too many. Can the friends unravel the secrets of the creature and the mysterious PezCorp before someone else dies?
